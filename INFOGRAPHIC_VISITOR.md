## TYPOGRAPHY SUGGESTION

**Headers:** Bold, Sans-serif (18-24px)
**Body:** Regular, Sans-serif (14-16px)
**Step Numbers:** Extra Large, Bold (48-64px)
**Icons:** 56-72px size

## LAYOUT OPTIONS

### Option 1: Vertical Flow
- Stack all 6 steps vertically
- Arrows pointing down
- Best for mobile viewing

### Option 2: 2x3 Grid
- Two columns, three rows
- Numbered boxes
- Better for print/poster

### Option 3: Circular Flow
- Steps arranged in circle
- Numbers around edge
- Visually engaging
