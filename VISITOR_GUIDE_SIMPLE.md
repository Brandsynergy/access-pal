### Camera Not Working?
- Make sure you tapped "Allow" for camera access
- Try refreshing the page
- Use Firefox on Android or Safari on iPhone

### Homeowner Not Answering?
- Wait up to 30 seconds

- Make sure you have internet connection
- Try scanning the QR code again
